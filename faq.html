Frequently Asked Questions

What is included in the Handover Checklist? +
Once the project is completed and the final balance is settled, we provide a structured handover to give you full control. This includes:
  • Full GitHub Access: We transfer the repository to your account. You own 100% of the source code.
  • Netlify Dashboard: Ownership of the hosting team and site settings is moved to you.
  • Domain Management: Full login credentials for your professional .com or .ph name.
  • Security Setup: We help you enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA) for your new accounts.
  • Deployment Config: Your site is set to update automatically whenever code changes are pushed.
Will my website show up on Google? +
Yes! We register your website on Google Search Console so it appears in search results.

Note on Ads: While we ensure you are "on the map," paying for "Sponsored" or "Ad" spots is a separate marketing budget handled directly through Google Ads.
How does the 50% down payment work? +
To start, we require a 50% deposit. In return, we provide a Live Preview Link. You can watch the development progress daily and give feedback in real-time. The remaining 50% is only due once you are 100% satisfied with the final build.
How long does it take? +
Typical builds take 7–14 days depending on the complexity and how quickly we receive your brand content.
Do you provide hosting? +
Yes! Our Identity Pack includes managed hosting. For the Essential Pack, we help you set up your own free Netlify account so you never have to pay a "middleman" fee for hosting.
Is it mobile-friendly? +
Absolutely. Every website we build is "Mobile-First," meaning it is designed to look perfect on smartphones, tablets, and desktops.
