Got Questions?

Transparent answers about your future website.

Will my website show up on Google? +
Yes, it will show. We submit your sitemap to Google Search Console so your business appears in organic search results.

Important Note: Registration for Google Ads (the "Sponsored" spots) is not included, as this requires a separate budget paid directly to Google.
Do I have to pay monthly fees? +
It depends on your chosen package:
  • ✅ The Identity Pack: Includes hosting and domain registration for the first year.
  • ✅ The Essential Pack: We help you set up Free Hosting via Netlify. You only pay the direct cost of your domain annually.
Do I need to provide the photos and text? +
Ideally, yes. You know your business best! However, we can use high-quality stock images for free if you don't have professional photos yet.
Do I own the website? +
100% Yes. Unlike other agencies that "lock" you in, we provide full access to your GitHub repository and hosting accounts. You own the code.
