Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take? +
7–14 days depending on content.
Is it mobile-friendly? +
Yes, fully responsive.
Can I upgrade later? +
Yes, anytime.
Do you provide hosting? +
Yes! Our Identity Pack includes one year of free hosting. To ensure your site stays secure and runs smoothly, we manage the hosting environment for you during this period.

Regarding Ownership: While we manage the server for technical stability, you always own your website’s content and design. If you prefer to manage your own hosting from the start, or wish to move to your own server later, we provide a full account turnover and assist with the migration.
Can I request custom designs? +
Absolutely! We tailor websites to your brand and ideas.
Do you offer domain registration? +
Yes! A custom domain name registration is included for the first year in our Identity Pack and higher tiers.

Please note: While standard domain extensions (like .com or .ph) are included, the final availability and price may vary depending on your preferred name. If you choose a premium domain that carries a higher registration cost, we will settle the price difference with you before proceeding.
What payment methods do you accept? +
We aim to make the process as convenient as possible for local business owners. We currently accept:
  • GCash (Digital Wallet)
  • Cash (Payable upon project turnover)
To begin your custom project, we typically require a 50% down payment, with the remaining balance due once the website is completed and ready for turnover.
Can I provide my own content? +
Yes, we can use your images, text, and brand assets.
Do you offer ongoing support? +
Yes! We don’t just build your site and leave. We offer flexible maintenance and support packages at a very low service rate.

Whether you need small content updates, security checks, or technical troubleshooting, we are available to ensure your website continues to run perfectly as your business grows.
