Frequently Asked Questions

Are the live demos the exact website I will get? +
No. Our live demos are inspiration references to show the quality and layout possibilities. Every project we build is a custom experience designed specifically for your unique brand and ideas.
Will my website show up on Google? +
Yes! We optimize the structure of your site and register it on Google so it appears in natural (organic) search results.

Regarding Google Ads: Paying for "Sponsored" or "Ad" spots (PPC) is a separate marketing cost handled directly through Google. We build the high-quality website first, so that when people find you—whether through search or ads—they see a professional brand they can trust.
How long does it take? +
Typical builds take 7–14 days depending on the complexity and how quickly we receive your specific brand content and feedback.
Do you provide hosting and domains? +
It depends on your package:

Essential Pack: You register your own domain. We build the site on your GitHub/Netlify accounts so you have direct control over your renewals with no middleman markup.

Identity Pack: We handle everything. Custom domain registration and managed hosting are included in the package.
What payment methods do you accept? +
We accept GCash or Bank Transfer.

To begin your project, we require a 50% down payment, with the remaining balance due once the website is completed and ready for the final turnover.
Who owns the accounts and code? +
You do! Once the project is finished and paid for, we transfer the GitHub and Netlify login credentials directly to you. You are the 100% legal owner of your site's code and can manage it forever without being locked into our services.
Is the website mobile-friendly? +
Absolutely. Every build we create is fully responsive, meaning it automatically adjusts to look perfect on phones, tablets, and desktops.
